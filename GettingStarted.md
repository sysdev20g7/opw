# Getting started

When starting to explore the project, looking at the `ObjectController.cs` is a good
place to start. Other important classes are located in the **Core**-folder, see below. 

### Directory structure
```
Assets/
├── 3rdParties
├── Animation
├── Materials
├── Music
├── Palettes
├── Prefabs
├── Scenes
└── Scripts
    ├── Combat
    ├── Core
    │   ├── CameraController.cs
    │   ├── DayController
    │   ├── Helper.cs
    │   ├── ObjectController.cs
    │   ├── Save
    │   └── SceneManagement
    ├── Enemy
    ├── Health
    ├── Player
    └── UI
```
